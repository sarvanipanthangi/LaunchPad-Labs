

# LauchPad Labs

> *Your career doesn’t need advice — it needs a co-pilot.*

An AI-powered career intelligence platform that analyzes your profile, compares it with real-time industry demand, and generates a personalized 30-day roadmap to bridge your skill gap.

---

## Problem

Students and early professionals:

* Don’t know what skills they’re missing
* Follow random roadmaps
* Get generic advice
* Lack data-backed direction

---

## Our Solution

Career Co-Pilot acts as an AI navigation system for your career:

**Profile → Skills → Market → Gap → Plan**

We:

1. Extract skills from your resume/LinkedIn
2. Compare them with real job market data
3. Identify measurable skill gaps
4. Generate a structured 30-day learning roadmap
5. Continuously improve the plan based on your progress

---

## Key Features

*  Resume / LinkedIn parsing
*  Skill strength visualization (technical + soft skills)
*  Dream role selection
*  Market demand analysis
*  Skill gap comparison (You vs Market)
*  AI-generated 30-Day Learning Plan
*  Dynamic re-planning based on progress
*  Self-review AI critique panel

---

## Datasets Used

* Hugging Face Skills Extraction Dataset
* LinkedIn Job Postings Dataset (Kaggle)

---

## Tech Stack

**Frontend:** React / Next.js, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion
**Backend:** Python (FastAPI), NLP pipelines
**Data:** Market job postings + skill extraction models

---

## What Makes This Different

This isn’t a resume checker.

It’s a **career navigation engine** that:

* Uses real market trends
* Shows measurable career distance
* Builds execution-ready learning plans
* Improves its own plan
